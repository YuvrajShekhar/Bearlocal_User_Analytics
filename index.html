🎯 How Profitability Score is Calculated

The profitability score combines three key performance indicators to identify the most valuable team members:

Profitability Score = (40% × Volume) + (30% × Efficiency) + (30% × Quality)
  • Volume (40%): Total connected outbound calls compared to team maximum
  • Efficiency (30%): Calls per hour worked compared to team maximum
  • Quality (30%): Connection rate percentage compared to team maximum
